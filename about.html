welcome to dentocare

we create beautiful smiles

At DentoCare, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive dental care that seamlessly blends clinical excellence with a compassionate, patient-first approach. Our highly skilled team of dental professionals brings years of experience and a deep commitment to oral health, ensuring that every patient receives attentive, ethical, and personalized care.

We leverage modern dental technology and evidence-based treatment methods to deliver precise diagnoses and effective solutions tailored to individual needs. At DentoCare, our goal is not just to treat dental concerns, but to build lasting relationships and help you maintain a healthy, confident smile you can truly trust.

Dr. Arav Mehta

Prosthodontist Specialist

Dr. Arav Mehta specializes in restoring smiles through advanced prosthodontic care. With extensive experience in dental restorations, crowns, and implants, he focuses on functional precision, aesthetic balance, and long-term comfort, helping patients regain confidence and optimal oral function through personalized treatment plans.

Dr. Naveen Joshi

Orthodontist Specialist

Dr. Naveen Joshi is dedicated to improving dental alignment and bite balance using modern orthodontic techniques. He emphasizes comfort, accuracy, and long-term results, guiding patients through customized orthodontic treatments that support healthier smiles, improved function, and enhanced confidence at every stage of care.

Dr. lucy Collins

Endodontist Specialist

Dr. Lucy Collins focuses on advanced endodontic treatments, including root canal therapy and infection management. Known for her gentle approach, she prioritizes patient comfort while delivering precise, pain-conscious care aimed at preserving natural teeth and ensuring long-term oral health outcomes.

Dr. Sara Malik

Periodontist Specialist

Dr. Sara Malik specializes in periodontal care, focusing on gum health and preventive treatments. With a strong emphasis on early detection and personalized care, she helps patients maintain healthy gums, manage periodontal conditions, and support the foundation of long-term dental wellness.
